Top Story

A STRUCTURED GROTESQUE SANS-SERIF FONT BASED ON THE CHICAGO SKYLINE

Top Story’s...Story

Top Story was inspired by a book series written by Jim Butcher which is set in modern day Chicago, but draws heavily on Chicago's 1930's gangster heritage. Top Story is a grotesque sans-serif typeface with a low x-height and tall capitals which emphasize the height difference and imitate Chicago's skyline. The font has a solid, masculine structure, yet a slight slant to the capitals give it a dangerous air.
